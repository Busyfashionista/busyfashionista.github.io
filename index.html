Who we are



Busy Fashionista is part of the reinvented luxury generation: the sharing lifestyle.

Our business gives women the choice of effortlessly accessible and affordable designer dresses to hire.

Dresses are hand-picked and the service is a personalised experience.

How it works


Busy Fashionista is appointment only. Please view Get In Touch for contact details.

You may want a quick lunch break appointment to pick up a last minute dress or organise a girls-night-in to try on various dresses and relax with a glass of champagne.

Once you find the perfect dress, it can be yours straight away or reserve for a future date.

Dresses are hired on a 4 days window and additional days are negotiable, should you be jet-setting to a dreamy location.

Worried about damaging the dress? We offer a non refundable insurance fee for your peace of mind.Payments can be done via credit card, cash or bank transfer.
